🌍 About Carbon Footprint

✅ What is Carbon Footprint?

A carbon footprint is the total amount of greenhouse gases (GHGs), primarily carbon dioxide (CO₂), emitted directly or indirectly by human activities. It is measured in kilograms (kg) or metric tons (tons) of CO₂ equivalent (CO₂e).

📌 Sources of Carbon Footprint

  • Transportation: Cars, buses, air travel, and public transport.
  • Electricity Usage: Home appliances, heating, cooling, and electronic devices.
  • Food Consumption: Meat, dairy, processed foods, and imported goods.
  • Household Activities: Water usage, waste generation, and shopping habits.

📊 How is Carbon Footprint Measured?

Carbon footprint is calculated based on energy consumed, distance traveled, and type of activity using standard emission factors.

Carbon Footprint = Activity Data × Emission Factor

🌱 How to Reduce Carbon Footprint?

  • ✅ Use Public Transport & Carpooling 🚆
  • ✅ Switch to Renewable Energy ☀️
  • ✅ Eat More Plant-Based Foods 🥦
  • ✅ Recycle & Reduce Waste ♻️
  • ✅ Use Energy-Efficient Appliances ⚡

📌 Global Carbon Footprint Facts
